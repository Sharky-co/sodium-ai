How Sodium AI Works

Sodium AI uses advanced machine learning to create personalized workout routines based on your unique needs, schedule, and fitness goals.

Data Collection

Sodium collects information about your fitness level, available time, equipment, and goals to create a comprehensive profile.

Analysis

The system analyzes your data against thousands of successful workout patterns to identify the most effective approach for you.

Adaptation

As you progress, the Sodium continuously adjusts your routine based on your performance and feedback to ensure optimal results.
